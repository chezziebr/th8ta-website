## PAGE OVERVIEW

This is TH8TA's signature content page—the one that differentiates us from every other apparel retailer. It explains the 4-Stage Recovery System™ in depth, provides scientific context, and helps customers understand exactly when to wear what.

4. Position TH8TA as recovery authorities
5. Drive conversion by removing confusion ("When do I wear this?")

**Target Audience:**
- New visitors trying to understand what TH8TA is
- Athletes mid-browse who need clarity on product organization
- Science-minded customers who want the "why"
- Anyone who's ever wondered "when do I actually wear this?"

## CONTENT GUIDELINES

### Voice Reminders
- **Confident but approachable** ("Here's what most athletes miss" not "You're doing it wrong")
- **Specific over vague** ("Post-long run in the car" not "after exercise")
- **Science-backed but accessible** (Explain parasympathetic without jargon)
- **A little sass, a lot of substance** ("Presentable AF" + real science)

### What to Avoid
- **Don't oversell** ("life-changing miracle gear")
- **Don't be preachy** ("you MUST follow all 4 stages")
- **Don't use medical claims** ("prevents injury" or "speeds healing")
- **Don't be vague** ("versatile comfort for any occasion")
